Dr. Sanjay Kishanrao Suryawanshi

M.V.Sc. Pharmacology & Toxicology.

He started his career in poultry industry in 2004 from Sneha farms. Later worked in Japfa & Suguna where he got a good exposure to various divisions, labs etc. Worked as manager Health & QC as well as QA. He got expertise in microbiology & nutrition divisions. He conducted various field trials in lab as well as on fields for different drugs for the betterment of industry. In 2010 he joined Mayuri group of companies. He established two new labs here. The breeder capacity was doubled in the first two years of tenure. His company got recognition as best performing in this sector with larger batch sizes. He made pivotal growth in integration division with many fold increase in placements. It's a leading company in terms of performance & quality. He made to start two new divisions in commercial layer with 3.5 lack capacity & a small pivotal dairy unit of 200 buffaloes. He has attended more than 100 technical sessions & seminars. He is a well-known technical person in the core of the industry with experiences in all divisions of poultry, large animals such as broiler breeding, commercial layers, broiler farming in health & nutrition both aspects with a specific recognition of problem solving.

Services rendered
  1. Microbiology lab - HA, HI, culture ABST, Health audit of farm, hatchery. Isolation & identification of pathogenic bacteria & fungi.
  2. Nutrition - wet chemistry analysis of feed & raw materials for various crucial parameters , cv test.
  3. Custom designing of vaccination schedule & feed formulations based on area & period with latest updates in least cost for better profitability.
  4. Managemental practices training for all farming.
  5. Problem solving.
